Online appointment booking for clients.

A simple and effective tool for managing customer records in beauty salons and other businesses

Opportunities

Bee o'clock

  • Automate records

    Forget about confusion with records. Our service automates customer onboarding processes, allowing you to focus on your work, not on record administration.

  • Reminder to clients

    Make sure your customers always remember their visit! Automatic SMS and email reminders will ensure that your clients arrive on time and reduce the number of missed appointments.

  • Customized for your business

    Make Bee o'clock a part of your brand! You can customize each interface of the service to your style and brand, which allows you to individualize your workflows.

  • Manage your records online

    All information is at your fingertips - you can access customer history, schedules, and records from any device. You can make decisions anywhere and anytime.

How does it work?

1

Registration on the platform

Create an account: Fill in basic information about your business, add address and contact details.

2

Service settings

Add your services: Specify the list of services, their duration and cost.

3

Customer invitations

Get a unique link to your record page and share it via website, social media, or email.

4

Online customer registration

Convenient booking: Customers book a service and a convenient time online, without calling or waiting.

5

Managing records

Control your schedule: Get notified about new recordings, view and manage your schedule in a convenient interface.

6

Analysis and development

Track your success: Analyze your record and customer activity statistics to improve your service.
